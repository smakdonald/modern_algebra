Exploration 12.1. Submodules and Zorn’s Lemma.

Let \(N\) be a submodule of an \(R\)-module \(M\text{.}\) Using Zorn’s Lemma, prove that there is a submodule \(N'\) such that
  1. \(N\cap N' = (0)\text{,}\) and
  2. \(N''\cap(N + N')\neq(0)\) for every non-zero submodule \(N''\) of \(M\text{.}\)
