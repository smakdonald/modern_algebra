Exercise 1.62.

  1. The exponential map \(\exp: ({\mathbb{R}}, +) \to (\mathbb{R}^\times, \cdot)\) is a homomorphism.
  2. The natural log map \(\ln: (\mathbb{R}^\times, \cdot) \to ({\mathbb{R}}, +)\) is a homomorphism.
  3. For any positive integer \(n\text{,}\) the map determinant map \(\det: \operatorname{GL}_n(\C) \to (\C^\times, \cdot)\) is a group homomorphism.
