Corollary 13.41. Every Vector Space has a Basis.

  1. Every \(F\)-vector space \(V\) has a basis.
  2. Every linearly independent subset of \(V\) is contained in some basis
  3. Every set of vectors that spans \(V\) contains some basis.

Proof.

  1. Apply the Theorem 13.40 with \(I = \emptyset\) and \(S = V\text{.}\)
  2. Apply the Theorem 13.40 with \(I\) arbitrary and \(S = V\)
  3. Apply the Theorem 13.40 with \(I = \emptyset\) and \(S\) arbitrary.
