Proposition 15.10. Equality of \(F[x]\)-Modules.

The two assignments \(M \mapsto (M|_F, \l_x)\) and \((V, g) \mapsto V_g\) defined above are mutually inverse: Given a \(F[x]\)-module \(M\text{,}\) there is an equality of \(F[x]\)-modules \(M = (M|_F)_{\l_x}\) and given a pair \((V, g)\) with \(V\) an \(F\)-vector space and \(g\) an \(F\)-linear operator on \(V\) we have an equality of pairs \(((V_g)|_F, \l_x) = (V,g)\text{.}\)
